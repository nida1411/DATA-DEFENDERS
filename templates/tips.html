🚨 CYBER SAFETY CENTER

Stay alert. Stay secure. Protect yourself from phishing attacks.

⚠️ Cyber criminals create fake websites every day to steal passwords, banking details, and personal information.

🔒 Check HTTPS

Always verify that websites use HTTPS encryption before entering passwords or personal information.

🚫 Avoid Suspicious Links

Never click unknown links from emails, messages, or popups claiming urgent account verification.

🧠 Watch for Fake Domains

Attackers often replace letters with numbers like amaz0n.com or paypa1.com to trick users.

🔑 Use Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication for important accounts.

📧 Beware of Phishing Emails

Do not trust emails asking for OTPs, passwords, or urgent account updates.

⚡ Report Suspicious Websites

If you discover fake websites or scams, report them immediately to prevent attacks.

🚨 Common Phishing Warning Signs

🔴 Urgent “verify your account” messages
🔴 Websites asking for passwords unexpectedly
🔴 Fake banking or payment pages
🔴 Misspelled domain names
🔴 Suspicious popups and redirects
🔴 Links shortened using unknown URL shorteners
